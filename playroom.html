Help Rules of Solitaire...
This Solitaire belongs to that amazing type of puzzle problems, which in their combinational capabilities are almost as good as chess puzzles. At the same time, the unfolding of this solitaire plays the role of emotional relaxation. This Solitaire involves the layout of cards according to certain rules: it is necessary to clean this card field, removing two cards of the same rank in one move - two deuces, two queens, etc. You can use the last cards of vertical columns only. To cancel the selected card, click on any other in the last rows.
